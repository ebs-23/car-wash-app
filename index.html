OUR SERVICES


Car Wash Programs

This includes complete vacuuming of cars including seats and boot, Tyre Arches cleaning, Underbody wash, Engine hot water / steam wash, Washing and cleaning of door frames, Body liquid polishing / waxing.

Interior Cleaning

From thorough vacuuming of carpets and upholstery to gentle yet effective stain removal treatments and special attention to high-touch areas like door handles,steering wheels,and dashboard surfaces,sanitizing them to ensure a hygienic environment.

Car Detailing

This is for cleansing and protection of windshield of cars. With this treatment, hard water marks and oxidation are removed and a fine protective layer is formed to provide clear vision for driving during the night as well as during the rainy season.

Ceramic Coating

This protective shield gives durable protection from all types of external factors including road salts, insect acids, oxidation, bird droppings, minor scratches, oxidation and helps in protecting the vehicle from damaging sun rays.
